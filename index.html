About the Institute

Shivam Institute for Vocational Training (SIVT) in Bangana offers vocational courses that equip learners with practical IT and career skills. Programs include NIELIT 'O' Level, basic computer courses, Tally, and short-term job-oriented trainings. The institute emphasizes hands-on labs, small batch sizes, and practical assessments.

Courses Offered

  • NIELIT 'O' Level (1 year) — Foundation IT diploma covering programming, web design, networking basics.
  • C-DAC 'DMCA'(1 year) — Foundation IT diploma covering programming, web design, networking basics.
  • Basic Computer (CCC) — Computer literacy course for beginners.
  • Tally & Accounting — Practical accounting and GST-ready bookkeeping using Tally.
  • Short-term Skill Courses — Web design, Python basics, MS Office, Digital Marketing (4–12 weeks).
  • Workshops & Seminars — Career skills, resume writing, interview preparation.
