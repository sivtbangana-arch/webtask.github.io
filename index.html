About the Institute

Shivam Institute for Vocational Training (SIVT) was established in November 2022 in Bangana to provide quality vocational and IT education. It offers certified courses like NIELIT ‘O’ Level, C-DAC DMCA, Tally, and Basic Computer Programs. The institute focuses on hands-on practical training and small batch learning for better skill development. Students gain real-world experience through projects, workshops, and practical assessments. SIVT is an Authorized Center of C-DAC and NIELIT, offering recognized government certifications. Its mission is to make affordable, industry-aligned education accessible to everyone. Courses are designed to build career-ready and job-oriented skills. The institute promotes digital literacy and self-reliance among youth. With dedicated faculty and modern labs, it ensures quality training and guidance. SIVT continues to empower the local community through education, skills, and opportunity.

Courses Offered

  • NIELIT 'O' Level (1 year) — Foundation IT diploma covering programming, web design, networking basics.
  • C-DAC 'DMCA'(1 year) — Foundation IT diploma covering programming, web design, networking basics.
  • Basic Computer (CCC) — Computer literacy course for beginners.
  • Tally & Accounting — Practical accounting and GST-ready bookkeeping using Tally.
  • Short-term Skill Courses — Web design, Python basics, MS Office (4–12 weeks).
  • Workshops & Seminars — Career skills, resume writing, interview preparation.
